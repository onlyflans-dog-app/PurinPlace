Purin's Place

You are at Purin's Place, use the kitchen to create flans.
This "game" is similar to picrew, but custom made for Inukai Purin Flans.
The "game" is inspired from the Purple Place cake making game.

Control:
Arrow Up - Switch Bodypart, or download
Arrow Down - Switch Bodypart, or throw in garbage
Arrow Left - Move flan to next step
Arrow Right - Move flan to previous step
